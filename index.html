economy simulation - rules:
- time: 20 turns (years)
- initial money: 6000 corns
- initial population: 100 citizens
- initial fields: 400 fields
- Every citizen eats 20 corns per year.
- The population increases by 1 citizen with 40 corns.
- Per field: 2 corns are needed for seeding.
- The annual random yield is 1 - 10 corns (1 year after seed).
- 1 citizen can cultivate 10 fields.
- The selling and purchase of fields takes place in the end of every turn.
- random field charge: 1 - 10 corns
- As long as there is corn money it is possible to bargain fields.
- If there is less than 1 field, less than 1 corn or less than 1 citizen the game is over.
